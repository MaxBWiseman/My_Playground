Whiskey Drop

Whiskey Drop is a whiskey subscription service that delivers a variety of whiskey samples to your door every month. We offer a variety of whiskey samples from around the world, so you can discover new and exciting whiskeys without having to commit to a full bottle.

How it Works

When you sign up for Whiskey Drop, you'll receive a box of whiskey samples every month. Each box contains a variety of whiskey samples from around the world, so you can discover new and exciting whiskeys without having to commit to a full bottle. You can choose from a variety of subscription plans, so you can find the one that works best for you.

You must be over 18 to sign up to our service. Please drink responsibly.
